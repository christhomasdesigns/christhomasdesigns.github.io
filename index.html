WHO'S CHRIS THOMAS?

With experience ranging from Associate Design Director to Lead UI/UX Designer, I am a multi-disciplinary designer who believes that a brand isn't just how something looks, it's how it works.

I ride the wave between high-level brand storytelling and tactical digital product design. My career has been defined by agility, floating fluidly from the fast currents of startup incubators to the deep end of global tech brands.

When im not tackling the big waves, I am skim boarding my local communities. A personal mission, I've dedicated the last decade to pro-bono design for local charities to help mission-driven organizations find their voice.
