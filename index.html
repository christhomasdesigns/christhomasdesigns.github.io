MY HISTORY

I am a multi-disciplinary designer who believes that a brand isn't just how something looks, it's how it works. With experience ranging from Associate Design Director to Lead UI/UX Designer, I bridge the gap between high-level brand storytelling and tactical digital product design.

My career has been defined by agility. Whether I'm navigating the fast-paced world of startup incubators or defining visual systems for global tech giants, I bring the same level of rigorous craft and strategic thinking.

Beyond the commercial, I am deeply committed to my community, having dedicated the last decade to pro-bono design for local charities to help mission-driven organizations find their voice.
