Intel Brand Systems

Client: Intel Corporation
Partner: Wolff Olins
Product: Intel Core, Intel Evo, Edge Computing
Scope: Master brand translation to sub-brand systems
Role: Associate Design Director

OVERVIEW:

A Legacy Brand Undergoing Transformation

Intel's rebrand marked a strategic shift from a component-led identity to a platform and ecosystem brand spanning consumer devices, enterprise infrastructure, AI, and edge computing.

I was part of a small team that helped win the partnership with Intel to develop a new logo, visual identity, and global design system capable of operating at massive scale. As part of the core rebrand team, I helped led brand experience design and system execution for Intel Core, Intel Evo, and Edge Computing (IoT), translating high-level brand strategy into clear, scalable, and usable brand experiences across audiences and touchpoints.

My role sat at the intersection of strategy and design direction with a focus on turning complexity into clarity.
