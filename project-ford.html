Ford Project

Transforming the future of automotive design and technology.

OVERVIEW:

FordPass: From Connected Ownership to an EV-Ready Digital Platform

As they transition into electric vehicles and connected services, their digital ecosystem needed to evolve alongside their products.

We partnered with Ford to create a design language that bridges their storied heritage with a bold, sustainable future. The challenge was to modernize without losing the trust and recognition that comes with over a century of innovation.

The result is a cohesive digital experience that spans vehicle interfaces, mobile applications, and web platformsâ€”all unified by principles of clarity, confidence, and American ingenuity.

WHAT WAS DONE

Developed a comprehensive digital design system spanning in-vehicle experiences, mobile apps, and web platforms. Created UI components, motion principles, and interaction patterns that reflect Ford's commitment to innovation.

THE WORK

Our approach focused on creating intuitive interfaces that enhance the driving experience without overwhelming users. We developed new information architectures, navigation patterns, and visual systems that work seamlessly across Ford's entire product ecosystem.

Every design decision was informed by extensive user research with Ford owners, test drives, and collaboration with engineering teams to ensure feasibility and performance.
